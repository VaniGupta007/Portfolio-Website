Project-1 (Prompt Engineering)📆

I explored the intersection of prompt engineering and digital marketing and it turned out to be a really fun experiment.

• Created an AI-generated visual for “Spotify Wrapped 2026 – Coming Soon”
• Used prompt engineering to design it from scratch
• Treated it like a real digital marketing announcement

What started as just experimenting with prompts quickly became a creative exercise in marketing :

| Thinking about visuals
| Understanding brand vibe
| Crafting prompts to match a campaign style

Tools used in generating the image:
- Claude Chatbot
- Adobe Firefly
- Canva

This made me realize that prompt engineering is not just technical It can actually be a powerful tool for creativity, storytelling, and even marketing.
It gave me a new perspective on how AI can be used beyond just generating content and instead help in building ideas and campaigns.
